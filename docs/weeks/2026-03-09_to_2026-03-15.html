Week 15 · General Awareness

09 Mar – 15 Mar, 2026

7 topics summarised · 80 practice questions

01

RBI & Monetary Policy

  • ★ Security Receipts (SR) glide‑path – extended to FY 2027‑28 (circular 24‑Feb‑2025).
  • Urban Cooperative Banks (UCBs) – housing‑loan exposure limit raised to 25 % of assets (max ₹3 cr) (circular 24‑Feb‑2025).
  • UCB loan‑exposure ceiling lifted from ₹1 cr to ₹3 cr (circular 24‑Feb‑2025).
  • UCB PSL target reduced from 75 % to 60 % (circular 24‑Feb‑2025).
  • 12 % sub‑limit for women borrowers (₹2  lakh) removed (circular 24‑Feb‑2025).
  • Responsible Business Conduct direction for cooperative banks issued 28‑Nov‑2025.
  • Rural Cooperative Banks (RCBs) brought under Integrated Ombudsman Scheme (notification 07‑Oct‑2025).
  • Master Direction 28‑Nov‑2025 removed Gross‑NPA < 7 % & Net‑NPA < 3 % caps for cooperative banks.
  • Master Direction 04‑Dec‑2025 allowed StCBs/DCCBs to open up to 10 new branches without RBI prior approval.
  • Banking Regulation Act 2025 amendment capped board‑tenure of cooperative banks at 10 years.
