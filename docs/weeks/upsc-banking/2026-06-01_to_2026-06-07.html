02

Economy & Finance

  • • RBI Monetary Policy Decision (06‑08 Jun 2026) – The *Monetary Policy Committee (MPC)* kept the repo rate at 5.25 %, unchanged, while maintaining a neutral stance; the standing deposit facility (SDF) remained at 5.00 % and the marginal standing facility (MSF) at 5.50 %, signalling a cautious but stable liquidity environment.
  • • Govt. Debt Market – State Government Securities Auction (04 Jun 2026) – 15 state SGS auctions were held, raising a combined ₹14 800 crore, demonstrating the expanding fiscal ambition and the depth of the short‑term debt market.
  • • Predictive Survey on GDP Growth – The *National Statistical Office (NSO)* released provisional data projecting genuine GDP growth at 7.6 % for FY 2025‑26, up from 7.1 % in FY 2024‑25, underscoring sustained economic momentum.
  • • Fiscal Deficit – Budget Projections (2025‑26) – The Union Budget highlighted a projected fiscal deficit of 3.9 % of GDP, showing a slight easing from the prior year and reflecting initiatives like *Income‑Tax Act 2025* reforms.
  • • Inflation Outlook – CPI and WPI – The latest *Inflation Expectations Survey* (May 2026) indicated CPI inflation at 3.4 % in March, expected to rise to 5.1 % by 2026‑27, while WPI food grains remained below −3 %, demonstrating differential inflation dynamics.
  • • Foreign Direct Investment Flow (June 2026) – FDI inflows of $15.6 billion were recorded, a modest increase over the last quarter, showing resilience despite global market uncertainties.
  • • SIDBI‑Run JV for MSME Support (05 Jun 2026) – The State Industries Development Bank of India announced a new small‑enterprise credit facility with an outlay of ₹12 crore, strengthening the financial cushion for MSMEs in the wake of supply‑chain disruptions.
  • • Electricity Sector – Renewable Capacity (08 Jun 2026) – National renewable installed capacity reached 250 GW, with a benchmark 50 % contribution to total power generation by 2030, reflecting progress on the *National Renewable Energy Policy* targets.
  • • Corporate Social Responsibility & Tax Incentives (04 Jun 2026) – *Central Government* unveiled a new tax credit scheme for firms investing ≥ ₹5 lakhs in green technology, incentivising corporate sustainability.
  • • Rural Employment Insurance Scheme (Rahulat) – *Launch of Rahulat* via the *NITI Aayog*’s scheme promising up to ₹15 k monthly for rural workers undergoing retraining, signaling an attempt to mop up the rising auto‑employment gaps.
  • • Rural Banking Digitisation (06 Jun 2026) – RBI’s *Citizen’s Charter* processing rate surged to 99.9 %, ensuring faster resolution of customer complaints via digital channels.
  • • International Remittance – SWIFT MT‑103 Fees – The RBI released a new *Inter‑bank Charging Protocol* capped at ₹120 per transaction for foreign remittances, reducing cross‑border cost burdens.
