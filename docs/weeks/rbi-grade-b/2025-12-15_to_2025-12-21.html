RBI Grade B · Week 03 · General Awareness

15 Dec – 21 Dec, 2025

7 topics summarised · 80 practice questions

01

RBI & Monetary Policy

  • ★ Monetary‑Policy Committee (MPC) – No new repo‑rate decision in this week; repo rate remains 6.50 % (target band 4 %‑6 %).
  • ★ Financial Stability Report (FSR) 2025 – Gross non‑performing assets (GNPA) ratio for scheduled commercial banks fell to 2.8 %, down from 3.9 % in FY 2024‑25.
  • Model Education Loan Scheme (MELS) – Allows collateral‑free education loans up to ₹ 7.5 lakh for students; amended on 21 Mar 2024.
  • Prudential Framework for Resolution of Stressed Assets (2025‑directions) – Principle‑based early‑recognition framework for commercial banks.
  • RBI circular on Urban Co‑operative Banks (UCBs) – All UCBs must adopt board‑approved loan policies (2024).
  • Digital Rupee (e‑Rupee) pilot – Continuing trial in selected B2B payments; no new rollout announced.
  • GS – GNPA trend – GNPA for education loans fell from 7 % (FY 2020‑21) to 2 % (FY 2024‑25).
  • Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation (DICGC) – Coverage raised to ₹ 5 lakh per depositor (including co‑ops).
02

Banking & Financial Sector

  • ★ SPARSH – Defence Pension Platform – 31.69 lakh defence pensioners (India + Nepal) onboarded; grievance‑redress time cut to 17 days (Nov 2025).
  • A‑DB Loans to India – Total > $2.2 bn for five projects (IT upgrade $846 mn, Rooftop Solar $650 mn, etc.).
  • Atal Pension Yojana (APY) – 8.45 crore subscribers as of 30 Nov 2025; majority in ₹1,000 slab (86.91 %).
  • Sahakar DigiPay & DigiLoan – UPI‑based digital payment and lending platform for Urban Co‑operative Banks launched 16 Dec 2025.
  • Quality‑Control Order (QCO) – Textiles – Export obligation under Advance Authorization extended to 18 months; cotton import duty exemption till 31 Dec 2025.
  • NBFC education‑loan GNPA – Declined to 2 % in FY 2024‑25 (from 7 %).
  • Sahakar Sarathi (Shared Service Entity) – Capital ₹ 1,000 cr; 33.33 % each of NABARD, NCDC, Rural Co‑ops.
  • National Cooperative Policy 2025 – Digital registrar, women‑led SME target 50 %.
  • PM KUSUM – Solar pumps – Land‑lease income ₹ 80,000 /ha/yr, diesel saving ≈₹ 60,000 /yr.
  • Viability Gap Funding (VGF) for Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) – 13 220 MWh funded ₹ 3,760 cr, 30 GWh funded ₹ 5,400 cr.
  • PLI – Advanced Chemistry Cell (ACC) Battery – Outlay ₹ 18,100 cr, target 50 GWh capacity (10 GWh grid‑scale).
