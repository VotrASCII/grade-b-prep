Week 11 · General Awareness

09 Feb – 15 Feb, 2026

7 topics summarised · 80 practice questions

01

RBI & Monetary Policy

  • No RBI‑specific monetary‑policy releases were issued in this week.
  • ★ RBI circular on Urban Cooperative Banks (UCBs) – 10 Feb 2026 – housing‑loan limit raised from 10 % → 25 % of total advances; new branch‑opening permission for UCBs.
  • ★ RBI directive – 10 Feb 2026 – DICGC insurance cover fixed at ₹5 lakh per depositor per bank (principal + interest).
  • ★ Credit‑risk classification of UCBs – ‘tier‑2’ UCBs now permitted to open up to 5 new branches per year.
  • ★ Co‑operative sector surcharge – income‑tax surcharge on societies with turnover ₹1 cr – ₹10 cr reduced from 12 % → 7 % (effective FY 2022‑23).
  • RBI participation in National Cyber Fraud Conference (10‑11 Feb 2026) – focus on mule‑accounts, SIM‑misuse, AI‑driven investigations.

  • CCI activity 2025 – 54 anti‑competitive matters, 38 final orders, 149 merger filings (146 disposed).
  • Competition (Amendment) Act 2023 – penalties based on global turnover; “Green Channel” introduced for deemed‑approval.
  • Post Office Savings Bank (POSB) – >45 crore account‑holders; Core Banking Solution now supports NEFT/RTGS, internet & mobile banking, e‑KYC.
  • e‑Voting (EVM/VVPAT) outreach – 1.20 lakh electors visited demo centres; 29 491 polling stations reached (as on 10 Feb 2026).
  • Digital Services for POSB schemes – internet banking enabled for NSC, KVP and SCSS.

  • India’s GDP growth forecast (RBI SPF) – 7.2 % for FY 2026‑27 (latest RBI projection).
