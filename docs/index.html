How it works

i

Exam-specific sources

Each exam is built from the notifications it actually draws on — PIB and RBI for RBI Grade B, broad PIB and the Economic Survey for UPSC, and so on.

ii

Descriptive summaries

The raw material is condensed into explanatory notes — what happened, the figures that matter, and why it is relevant — grouped by GA topic.

iii

Pattern-tuned practice

Every week ships exam-style MCQs whose topic and question-type mix is weighted from that exam's recent papers, attempted in the browser with instant scoring.
